Build a complete, professional, multi-section single-page website for "Dhrona AI" — an AI agency startup based in India that delivers end-to-end AI solutions across industries including Healthcare, Defense, Education, Salons, Schools, Retail, Finance, and more. The website should feel premium, futuristic, and trust-inspiring — like a top-tier global AI consultancy, not a template.

---

## DESIGN DIRECTION

**Visual Identity:**
- Dark background (#0A0A0F) with deep navy sections (#0D1117)
- Primary accent: Electric blue (#3B82F6) with subtle cyan (#06B6D4) highlights
- Secondary accent: Soft white (#F1F5F9) for headings, (#94A3B8) for body text
- Gold/amber (#F59E0B) as a trust/premium highlight used sparingly (badges, borders)
- Zero use of generic gradients — keep it clean, sharp, and bold

**Typography:**
- Display headings: "Space Grotesk" or "Outfit" — bold, modern, geometric
- Body text: "Inter" — clean and readable
- All font sizes should follow a clear scale: Hero H1 (64–72px), Section H2 (40–48px), Card H3 (22–24px), Body (16–18px), Caption (13–14px)
- Letter spacing on headings: slightly wide (-0.02em to 0.01em)

**Layout & Motion:**
- Clean grid layout with generous whitespace
- Subtle scroll-triggered fade-in animations on each section
- Hover micro-interactions on all cards and buttons
- No clutter — each section has one clear purpose

## OVERALL REQUIREMENTS
- Fully responsive: mobile, tablet, desktop
- Smooth scroll between sections
- Section IDs for anchor navigation
- Professional micro-animations (fade-in on scroll, hover effects on cards and buttons)
- All text must be readable — never sacrifice readability for style
- No stock-photo imagery — use abstract AI-themed SVG graphics, geometric shapes, and icon-based visuals
- Fast loading — optimize all assets
- Color accessibility: all text must pass contrast ratio standards
